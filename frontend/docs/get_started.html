Getting Started

GET STARTED

Visit our website www.pricebeta.com to access our services. We are a service provider in comparing prices of mobile phones, laptop and accessories across numerous online e-commerce stores. so getting started is as easy as reading our policies, term and conditions, privacy and about us, as all of these contain vital information on our business ethics, offers and limitations

Open an account

For a new account click on register and input ypur details, after creation your details will be automatically saved inacase of next visit. If you happen to log out during the first creation of the account you’d need to sign in during the second visit inputting your details.

Compare prices

Compare product prices across platforms by clicking the “price from...” button and user will be automatically redirected to the e-commerce platform that sells the product you can then make your purchase there.
