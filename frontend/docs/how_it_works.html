How does it works

HOW DOES IT WORK

As a User all you need to do is open an account with Pricebeta, subscribe to our newsletter channel to get updated prices on products across your favorite online stores. visit the site whenever you are in doubt of a certain product’s price or whenever you are looking for a comfortable price that meets your budget all you need to do is type the name of the product in the search bar and the product will pop up with the price it is going for in different online store

Browse categories

Now that you’ve gotten started with Pricebeta you can easily find your choice product by clicking the dropdown arrow on the browse category option, there you’d find numerous options of gadgets and elctronics to pick from.

Select choice product

Select your favorite item and price that meets your budget by clicking on the respective link which will take you to the page to make your purchase, you can also add to cart.
